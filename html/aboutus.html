About us

Super Traders is an organization that was started in 1997 by Mr.Haji Saleem Sharif( Popat ) to serve the market with the best of grocery products at very reasonable rates. He worked at HALT & Co in the initial days of his career Then managed to get a partner started NATHANI TRADING CO in 1986. Then he branched out as Asian Traders in 1991 and in 1996 he parted ways with his partners Asian Traders & started Super Traders in 1997. He was well known all around the food grains market in south India and was respected for his appetite for well-calculated risk and growth. Slowly he became a tycoon with great business acumen and expanded the product profile and market reach. Super traders now deal in over 1000+ SKU’s and have everything a household will need in terms of Grocery products and source these directly from the farmers at the APMC. Thereby ensuring the genuine quality of products sourced. Later the company ventured into the branded grocery segment under the brand ST and later on launched Multiple brands like

  • ST HIND,
  • ST DECCAN,
  • ST SUPER EXPRESS
  • & ST
